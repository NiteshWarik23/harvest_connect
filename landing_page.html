Why Choose Harvest Connect?

The Problem

  • Unfair Compensation: Farmers often receive only a fraction of what consumers pay
  • Quality Compromise: Multiple middlemen result in less fresh produce and higher prices
  • Lack of Transparency: Consumers have no visibility into how their money supports (or doesn't support) producers
  • Environmental Impact: Traditional supply chains create excessive carbon footprints and food waste

Our Solution

  • Transparent Supply Chain: Currently sourcing from trusted vendors while building direct farmer relationships
  • Fair Contribution: 10% of every purchase supports our Farmer Support Fund
  • Traceable Impact: See exactly how your purchases make a difference
  • Reward-Based Loyalty: Earn points with every purchase that translate to real value

For every order you place, we automatically contribute 10% of the order value to our Farmer Support Fund. While we currently source from trusted vendors and markets, this fund is already making an impact by providing:

  • Training programs for modern agricultural practices
  • Interest-free loans for farm expansion and direct partnerships

Frequently Asked Questions

How does my purchase actually help farmers?

10% of every purchase goes to our Farmer Support Fund, which directly supports farming communities through fair compensation, equipment investments, training programs, and interest-free loans. We're transparent about exactly where your money goes with our impact tracking system.

Do you source directly from farmers?

Currently, we source our produce from trusted vendors and markets while building relationships with farmers. Our goal is to transition to a direct farm-to-consumer model as we grow. Even now, a portion of every purchase goes to supporting farmers through our Farmer Support Fund.

How does the rewards program work?

You earn 1 point for every $1 spent. These points can be redeemed for free products, discounts on future orders, or even direct contributions to farming projects. Points never expire as long as your account remains active.

How fresh is your produce?

We pride ourselves on freshness! Our logistics system ensures that produce arrives at your doorstep within 48 hours of being received from our suppliers. This is significantly faster than traditional grocery supply chains.

What areas do you deliver to?

We currently deliver to major metropolitan areas with plans to expand. Enter your zip code on our app to check if we service your area. We're constantly growing our delivery network!

Is there a minimum order amount?

Yes, our minimum order is $20, which helps us ensure efficient delivery routes and maximize our environmental efficiency while maintaining fair compensation for our delivery partners.

How can I track my impact?

Your personal impact dashboard in our app shows exactly how much you've contributed, which farmers have benefited, and what projects your money is supporting. We believe in complete transparency.

Can I choose which farmers to support?

While our current system allocates support automatically, we're developing features that will allow you to direct your contributions to specific farming communities or projects in the future.

Would You Shop With Us?

We're building a community of conscious consumers who care about quality produce and supporting local farmers.
